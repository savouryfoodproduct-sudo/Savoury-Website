Our Story

Crafted in the Heart of Sri Lanka

Nestled in the lush landscapes of Pitigala, Sri Lanka, Savoury was founded with a passion for preserving the authentic taste of premium Ceylon tea.

Every leaf is carefully handpicked and expertly crafted in small batches to deliver exceptional freshness, rich aroma, and natural flavor.

Inspired by generations of Sri Lankan tea heritage, we bring the essence of nature to every cup.

"Every cup tells a story of Sri Lanka's finest tea gardens."
